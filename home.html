Bengal Institute of Technology is a private institute located in Kolkata. The institute is recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). Moreover, the Bengal Institute of Technology is affiliated with Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT).


Bengal Institute of Technology provides undergraduate programme in various specialisations. These specialisations include Electronics and Communication Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Information Technology, Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning), Biotechnology, etc. Furthermore, the top recruiters include Accenture, Cognizant, Capgemini, Intel, Infosys, PWC, TCS, etc. Below are the major highlights of the Bengal Institute of Technology:
